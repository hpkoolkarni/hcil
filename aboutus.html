About Us

We have truly come to believe that if you create something from your core, something undiluted, something inspiring, it is bound to find the right audience.e take pride in what goes into making our products and sharing it with the world. That’s the reason only the good goes in.Bagana’s story started in 2009 at a private family event when colourful handbags designed by Mrs.ABC for the attending guests became an instant hit! Nudged by her friends to continue creating these chic handbags, She approached her brother, to help her cope with the incoming orders. Finding a clear gap in the market for high-profile bags, the brother-sister duo decided to establish a world-class brand. They travelled through vibrant landscapes across Europe and Asia to find design inspirations and source the finest materials.
